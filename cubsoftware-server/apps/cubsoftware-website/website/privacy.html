Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

CUB SOFTWARE is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our services, including:

  • Social Media Saver: Download photos and videos from social media platforms
  • StreamerBot Commands: C# command collection for streamers
  • File Converter: Convert files between formats
  • Image Editor: Online image editing tools
  • PDF Tools: PDF manipulation utilities
  • QR Code Generator: Create custom QR codes
  • Text Tools: Text formatting and analysis utilities
  • Color Picker: Color selection and palette tools
  • CubVault: Secure password manager
  • QuestCord: Discord RPG bot

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

  • URLs you submit for downloading
  • Email address when you contact us

Automatically Collected Information:

  • IP address and browser type
  • Usage statistics (download counts)
  • Technical data for service improvement

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our services
  • To improve user experience and service functionality
  • To display aggregate statistics
  • To respond to support requests
  • To detect and prevent technical issues

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. Downloaded files are temporarily stored and automatically deleted after delivery.

5. Third-Party Services

Our services interact with third-party platforms (Instagram, TikTok, Twitter/X, Facebook, Discord, etc.) to retrieve content or provide functionality. Your use of these platforms is subject to their respective privacy policies.

6. CubVault Password Manager

CubVault is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • All passwords are encrypted locally on your device before any storage
  • We cannot access your master password or decrypted vault contents
  • Optional cloud sync uses end-to-end encryption - your data is encrypted before leaving your device
  • You maintain full control over your encrypted data

7. QuestCord Discord Bot

When using QuestCord, we collect:

  • Discord user ID and username for game progress tracking
  • Server ID for server-specific features
  • Game statistics (quests completed, currency earned, etc.)

We do not access or store your Discord messages, private information, or any data beyond what is necessary for game functionality.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We may use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

9. Data Retention

We retain minimal data necessary for service operation. Temporary files are automatically deleted after download completion. Statistics are stored in aggregate form without personally identifiable information.

10. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access information we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of data collection where applicable
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

12. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

13. Contact Us

For privacy concerns or questions, contact us at cubsoftware@outlook.com

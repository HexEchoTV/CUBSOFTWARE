Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

This Privacy Policy applies to all services provided by CUB SOFTWARE, including our website (cubsoftware.site), URL shortener (cubsw.link), Discord bots, and all online tools and applications (collectively, the "Services").

By using our Services, you consent to the data practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Services.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • URLs and Content: URLs you submit for downloading, shortening, or processing
  • User-Generated Content: Resumes, invoices, sticky notes, server templates, and other content you create
  • Account Information: When authenticating via Discord OAuth, we receive your Discord user ID, username, avatar, and guild (server) list
  • Communications: Email address and message content when you contact us
  • Reports: Information you provide when submitting reports through our report system

3.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • IP Address: Your IP address is collected for security, abuse prevention, rate limiting, and analytics purposes
  • Browser Information: Browser type, version, and user agent string
  • Device Information: Operating system and device type
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, download counts, click statistics on shortened links
  • Timestamps: Date and time of your activities
  • Referrer Information: The website that referred you to our Services

3.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Discord: User ID, username, discriminator, avatar URL, and guild list (when you authenticate)
  • Social Media Platforms: Publicly available media metadata when you use our download tools

4. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, operate, and maintain our Services
  • Improvement: To understand how users interact with our Services and improve functionality
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues, fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Abuse Prevention: To enforce rate limits, prevent spam, and ban malicious users
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes
  • Statistics: To generate aggregate, anonymized statistics about Service usage

5. Link Shortener (cubsw.link)

When you use our Link Shortener service:

  • We store the original URL you submit and the generated short code
  • We log your IP address to prevent abuse and enforce rate limits
  • We track click statistics (number of clicks) on shortened links
  • IP addresses associated with link creation are retained for security purposes
  • This data may be shared with law enforcement if required by law or if we suspect illegal activity
  • Links created using our service may be reviewed and removed if they violate our Terms of Use

Important: If you create links to malicious, illegal, or harmful content, your IP address and link data may be reported to appropriate authorities.

6. Discord Bots (QuestCord & CleanMe)

6.1 QuestCord

When you use QuestCord, we collect and store:

  • Discord user ID and username for player identification
  • Server ID for server-specific features
  • Game statistics: quests completed, currency earned, inventory, levels, achievements

We do NOT access or store your Discord messages, private information, or any data beyond what is necessary for game functionality.

6.2 CleanMe

When you use CleanMe, we collect and store:

  • Server ID for identifying saved configurations
  • Server structure data: role names, channel names, category names, and permission settings (when you use /save)
  • User ID of the person who saved the configuration

We do NOT access or store your Discord messages, member lists, message history, or any content beyond server structure. Saved configurations may be shared with other users via the CleanMe Website.

7. CleanMe Website (Server Template Sharing)

When using the CleanMe website:

  • Discord OAuth authentication provides us with your user ID, username, and avatar
  • We store server configurations you submit (server ID, channel/role/category structures)
  • Your Discord user ID is associated with servers you submit
  • Voting history is tracked to prevent duplicate votes
  • Submitted server templates are publicly visible to other users

You may delete your submitted servers at any time through your dashboard.

8. CubVault Password Manager

CubVault is designed with privacy as a core principle:

  • All passwords and sensitive data are encrypted locally on your device using your master password
  • We CANNOT access your master password or decrypt your vault contents
  • If you enable optional cloud sync, your data is encrypted BEFORE leaving your device (end-to-end encryption)
  • We have no ability to recover your data if you forget your master password
  • Your encrypted vault data is stored locally in your browser's localStorage

9. Shareable Content

Some Services allow you to create shareable links:

  • Sticky Board: Board data is encoded directly in the URL and is not stored on our servers. Anyone with the link can view the content.
  • Resume Builder: Shared resumes are stored on our servers and accessible via unique links. Anyone with the link can view the resume.
  • Countdown Maker: Shared countdowns are stored on our servers with view counts tracked.

When you share content via these services, you accept that the content becomes accessible to anyone with the link.

Types of cookies we use:

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect Service functionality.

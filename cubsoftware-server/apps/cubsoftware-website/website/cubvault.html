Features

Everything you need to keep your digital life secure.

End-to-End Encryption

Your passwords are encrypted locally before being stored. Only you can access them with your master password.

Your Data, Your Control

Your vault is stored on your device. Optionally enable encrypted cloud backup to sync across devices - your data is always encrypted before leaving your device.

Password Generator

Generate strong, unique passwords with customizable length, character sets, and complexity options.

Password Health Check

Identify weak, reused, and old passwords that need updating to keep your accounts secure.

Auto-Lock Security

Automatically locks after inactivity to protect your vault when you step away from your computer.

Clipboard Auto-Clear

Copied passwords are automatically cleared from clipboard after 30 seconds to prevent accidental exposure.
